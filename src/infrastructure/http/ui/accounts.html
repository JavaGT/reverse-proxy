Accounts

The invite secret is the same value as MANAGEMENT_REGISTRATION_SECRET on the server. Share it only with people who should create an account at /register.html. The field is masked; use Copy to place it on the clipboard.

Deleting a user removes their sessions and passkeys. You cannot delete your own account while signed in, or the only remaining user.

Your sign-in security uses the same APIs as /login.html: TOTP 2FA (QR setup, recovery codes) and WebAuthn passkeys. Turning off 2FA may require your password if your session is no longer “fresh”.
